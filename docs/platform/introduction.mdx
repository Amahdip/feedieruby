## Welcome to the SalamRuby Documentation!

SalamRuby is a versatile open-source platform for collecting and analyzing feedback from customers, users, and employees through targeted surveys. Whether you need simple forms or complex experience management solutions, SalamRuby scales with your needs.

This guide covers everything you need to set up, use, and develop with SalamRuby. You'll find step-by-step instructions, feature explanations, and best practices. It also includes advanced docs on extending SalamRuby using its self-hosted option, APIs, and SDKs.
